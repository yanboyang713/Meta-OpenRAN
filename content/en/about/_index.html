Meta-OpenRAN is a research platform for data-driven O-RAN experimentation

Building on top of OpenRAN Gym and OpenAI Cellular two projects, for data collection and RAN control. Have FIVE main parts:
  • Testbed Infrastructure
  • SMO (Service Management and Orchestration), including Non-real-time RIC
  • Near real-time RIC
  • NS3 (ns-O-RAN)
  • Open Scenario
