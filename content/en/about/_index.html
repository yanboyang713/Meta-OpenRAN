{{< /blocks/cover >}} {{% blocks/lead %}} Meta OpenRAN Testbed is a NC State University NICE-Lab project, supervied by Dr.Yuchen Liu. {{% /blocks/lead %}} {{< blocks/section >}}
Meta-OpenRAN is a research platform for data-driven O-RAN experimentation. Based on OpenRAN Gym and OpenAI Cellular two projects, building for data collection and RAN control. Have FIVE main parts: Testbed Infrastructure, SMO (Service Management and Orchestration), including Non-real-time RIC, Near real-time RIC, NS3 (ns-O-RAN), and Open Scenario.
